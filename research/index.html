Research Interests

  1. Statistical Interests:
    1. Functional and Distributional Data Analysis
    2. Modeling of Massive and Complex data
    3. High Dimensional Statistical Inference
    4. Nonparametric Statistical Methods
  2. Scientific Interests:
    1. Wearables
    2. Neuroimaging
    3. Health Sciences

I have broad research interests in theoretical and methodological research in Statistics motivated by fundamental applications in wearables, neuroimaging, and other related health sciences.
